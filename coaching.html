How to Record Your Gameplay

You will record a video of your gameplay using one of following (this is important because I need to see your mouse clicks):

  1. Outplayed (easiest - make sure to set your league settings to lowest for best performance)
  2. OBS/Streamlabs OBS/ some software that records your screen.
  3. Download from League client match history - I do not recommend this because I will not be able to see your clicks/camera. The file extension from the league client will be .rolf which you must convert to an mp4.

Once you have recorded your game, you will upload the game to Youtube as either a public or unlisted video and send me the video link via my Discord DM (@Step1v9) or email yaboystep@gmail.com.
