Tryndamere

The Barbarian King

Tryndamere is a melee fighter who excels at extended trades and split pushing. His ultimate ability, Undying Rage, allows him to survive for 5 seconds with 1 HP, making him a terrifying late-game threat.

Tips & Tricks

Early Game

  • Start with a Doran's Blade and a Health Potion.
  • Use your Q to heal when you're low on health.
  • Save your E for escaping ganks or chasing down enemies.
  • Build up your Fury before engaging in trades.
  • Focus on farming and getting to level 6 safely.

Mid Game

  • Start split pushing to create pressure on the map.
  • Use your ultimate when you're about to die in teamfights.
  • Build items that give you both damage and survivability.
  • Use your W to slow enemies when chasing or escaping.
  • Join teamfights only when necessary, prefer split pushing.

Late Game

  • Continue split pushing to force the enemy team to respond.
  • Use your ultimate strategically to survive key teamfights.
  • Build items that counter the enemy team's composition.
  • Use Teleport to join teamfights when needed.
  • Focus on taking objectives when the enemy team is distracted.
